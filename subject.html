Academic Subjects at Oxford

PPE

PPE

PPE combines Philosophy, Politics, and Economics to explore how societies function and how decisions shape the world.

Medicine

Medicine

The Medicine course focuses on human health, clinical skills, scientific research, and advanced medical knowledge.

Computer Science

Computer Science

Study algorithms, programming, AI systems, and problem-solving through modern computing technologies.

Law

Law

Law teaches legal principles, justice systems, and critical analysis of global legal frameworks.

English Literature

English Literature

English Literature explores poetry, novels, and drama from different periods, helping students build strong reading and analytical skills.

Philosophy

Philosophy

Philosophy studies ideas about knowledge, ethics, and reality, helping students think deeply, reason clearly, and analyze complex questions.

Engineering

Engineering

Engineering applies scientific and mathematical principles to design, build, and improve structures, machines, and modern technologies.

Psychology

Psychology

Psychology explores human behavior, thoughts, and emotions, helping students understand how people think and why they act the way they do.

Oxford Professors

Oxford University is home to world-renowned professors across diverse fields — from Philosophy, English Literature, and Politics to Medicine, Engineering, and Computer Science. They bring expertise, innovative research, and a passion for teaching, inspiring students to excel academically and creatively.

With decades of experience and international recognition, Oxford professors mentor students, drive groundbreaking research, and shape the future of global scholarship.
